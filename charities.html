Save the Children

Save the Children is a global charity that focuses on providing children with healthcare, education, and protection from harm. Their mission is to ensure that children grow up healthy, educated, and safe from exploitation and abuse.

Doctors Without Borders

Doctors Without Borders provides medical care to people affected by conflict, epidemics, disasters, and exclusion from healthcare. They offer emergency medical aid in more than 70 countries, regardless of race, religion, or political affiliation.

World Wildlife Fund (WWF)

The World Wildlife Fund is an international non-governmental organization dedicated to environmental conservation. WWF works on issues regarding the conservation of biodiversity, the reduction of human impact on the environment, and the promotion of sustainable development.

Habitat for Humanity

Habitat for Humanity is a global nonprofit organization working in local communities across all 50 states in the U.S. and in approximately 70 countries. The organization helps families build and improve places to call home, advocating for affordable housing and supporting sustainable and resilient building.

The Red Cross

The Red Cross is a humanitarian organization that provides emergency assistance, disaster relief, and education in communities around the world. It supports blood donations, disaster preparedness, and recovery efforts, ensuring aid reaches those in critical need.

Water.org

Water.org is a nonprofit organization dedicated to solving the global water crisis by providing access to safe water and sanitation. Co-founded by Matt Damon and Gary White, the organization uses innovative financing solutions like WaterCredit to empower communities and households to gain sustainable access to water and improve their living conditions.

Feeding America

Feeding America is the largest hunger-relief organization in the United States, operating a network of food banks and pantries to provide meals to millions of people facing food insecurity. Their mission is to ensure that no one goes hungry by addressing the root causes of hunger and advocating for policies to create a stronger safety net.

St. Jude Children's Research Hospital

St. Jude Children's Research Hospital is dedicated to advancing cures and means of prevention for pediatric catastrophic diseases through research and treatment. No family ever receives a bill from St. Jude for treatment, travel, housing, or food.

Oxfam International

Oxfam is a global organization that fights inequality to end poverty and injustice. They work to ensure people can improve their lives and livelihoods, access basic services, and respond to emergencies caused by climate change, conflict, and other crises.
